NSW COVID-19 Cases By Age Group

The bar chart below shows the NSW COVID-19 cases by age group.

This bar chart claims that the most COVID-19 patients are concentrated in adolescents aged 0-19 in NSW.

It may emphasise that teenagers prefer to play or study together. They may not willing to follow their parent’s teachings, and they don’t like staying at home.

As a result, they may become the main contagious. However, they have had better resistance due to a healthy body.

Tips! If you are a resident of NSW, please monitor your children at some special time, they may cause serious consequences.
